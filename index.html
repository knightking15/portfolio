SERVICES

The Services That I Provide

Dispensing Medications

Pharmacists accurately and safely dispense prescription medications to patients, ensuring that they receive the correct dosage and appropriate instructions for use.

Patient Counseling

Pharmacists provide valuable counseling to patients about their medications. They explain the purpose of the medication, potential side effects, and proper administration techniques.

Medication Monitoring

Pharmacists monitor patients' responses to medications and assess their overall therapeutic outcomes. They may perform lab tests, such as blood glucose monitoring for diabetes management or drug level monitoring for certain medications, to ensure that treatments are effective and safe.
